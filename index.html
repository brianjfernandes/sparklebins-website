About Me

I'm a 12-year-old entrepreneur running Sparkle Bins after school and on weekends. I started this business because I saw that most people don't have the time to clean their bins, and nobody wants to deal with that mess. So I decided to do it for them!

Every can I clean gets my full effort and attention. When I'm done, your bins will look (and smell!) like new. I always show up on time and make sure the job is done right.
